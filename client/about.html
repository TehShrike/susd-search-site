About

I love tabletop games, and I love Shut Up & Sit Down.

Besides watching all their new reviews, I watch episodes from their backlog and browse through their old videos.

The search on shutupandsitdown.com has a few niggling issues that kept bothering me:

  • It's slow
  • The images are large and take a lot of bandwidth if you're mobile
  • Browser navigation doesn't work (back/forward) and you can't send anyone a direct link to "all reviews" or "all episodes of The Opener"
  • There's no way to search through just the videos without searching the list of games or written reviews
  • There's no way to search by more than one tag, or by tags + "just videos", or tags + search term

So I made this site.

The source code is available at github.com/TehShrike/susd-search-site.
